Kingsman football academy is a world class football training academy situated in Westlands. It is specifically aimed at training young kids who have a talent in football and it will serve as an important tool to help them develop their talents. Most of the kids who graduate from this academy are scouted by teams in Italy and France.

Creative Kids


This is for kids who have a talent in football and also have an achievement accredited to their talent.They receive full sponsorship throughout their stay at Kingsman Football Academy and flights outside the country.

Upcoming Talent


This is for kids who are less than the age of 8. These are in this special category because they are really young but their interest in football is impressive. These kids have less intensive training (of course) but very wonderful sessions where each of them can showcase what they have learnt.

Part Time Trainees


This is a section specially for kids who are still pursuing education in school. We understand that there are those who cannot attend the academy 24/7 because of the commitment in school. Well, this is the right place for you. We allow them to come to the academy in the afternoons when they are free.
